Repair Services

In the marine and offshore industry, a product is only as good as its backup service. Karen Marine Global Trading has earned an outstanding reputation for providing 24-hour service and on-location repair services that efficiently solve problems and get its customers’ operations back up and running as quickly as possible. In fact, Karen Marine has an unsurpassed service network local technicians in most of country ports. So If anything ever happens to the spare parts we supplied, or if you need assistance during installing the marine spare part, we can send one of our local technicians to your vessel. The full range of our repair services includes 24-hour repair services, Vessel Inspection Service, Scheduled Preventive Maintenance, General Maintenance Projects, Diesel Engine Services, Complete engine overhauls on site, And many more...
Our highly skilled field service technicians arrive at each job site with all necessary tooling and factory-certified parts needed to perform the job. In addition, our technicians perform all repairs in strict compliance with OEM specifications and factory build procedures – while also following the latest engineering updates. All rebuilt components are thoroughly tested before being put into service – and our tools are regularly calibrated to ensure pinpoint accuracy. Peace of mind and the certainty that the spare parts are fitted correctly. Hence our clients can be sure that we are dedicated to providing excellent customer support and repair services with a focus on upholding the highest standards of quality. We will ensure your vessel is well maintained on the high seas by providing the best quality repair services and spare parts and keeping your costs down. That is what we call service.
